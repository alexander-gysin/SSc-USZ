Pipeline

The analysis is divided into modules:

Section Focus & Methods Description
1. Data Cleanup & QC Imports, cleans, and saves the raw data for downstream analysis.
2. Cohort Characterization Describes the cohort and assesses clinical parameter relationships.
x.1 Omics Variance PCA & VPA Assesses the global relationship between clinical parameters and proteins.
x.2 Omics Differential DEA & GSEA Shows differentially expressed proteins and pathways between groups.
x.3 Omics Predictive ML Models Finds patterns via UMAP, LASSO, and Random Forest.
x.4 Omics Linear Linear Models Correlates clinical parameters directly with Omics data.
